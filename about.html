About Mocha & Crumb Café

Our Story

Mocha & Crumb Café began in 2021 with a simple dream — to create a space where people can enjoy freshly brewed coffee, delicious homemade cakes, and genuine conversations. What started as a small family business has grown into a warm community hub for students, freelancers, and families.

Every cup we serve is made with care, using locally sourced coffee beans and fresh ingredients. We’re passionate about bringing people together over good food and great coffee.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable coffee and baked goods while creating a cosy, creative environment that inspires connection and productivity.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the freshest and finest ingredients.
  • Community: We believe in supporting local suppliers and events.
  • Comfort: We strive to make everyone feel at home in our café.
  • Creativity: We add love and artistry into every cup and cake.
